Recommended settings:
	1-character speedruns: 
		Start when generating a new seed, 
		Split on boss kills, treasure floors with boss skips, and victory room, 
		Reset on deaths.  
	
	4-character speedruns:
		Start when generating a new seed, 
		Split on Act III boss kills and victory room.  
	
	Ascension Climb:
		Start when generating a new seed, 
		Split for Ascension 0/Ascension 20 achievements and ascension progression.  
	
	All Achievements:
		Start when creating a new save slot, 
		Split for all Gem and Gem+ achievements except for Amethyst and both gems for your A20 climb character, 
		Split for Ascension 0, My Lucky Day, and Powerful achievements, 
		[optional] Split for Neon.
